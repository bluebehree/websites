About Pokémon Sun & Moon

Pokémon Sun & Pokémon Moon are the first set of Generation VII Pokémon games, coming for the Nintendo 3DS worldwide in 2016.

New feature!

Ultra Beasts

A mysterious presence threatening the Alola region--Ultra Beasts!

New Pokémon Forms!

Alolan Forms

Some Pokémon species have uniquely adapted to the microclimates of the Alola region, becoming what is known as a regional variant of their species.

Additional Challenges!

Island Challenge

The island challenge is a rite in which players travel through the four islands of Alola with the goal of becoming the strongest Trainer, known as the island challenge champion. In order to complete the island challenge, players must overcome each island's unique trials which may include, but are not limited to, finding items, completing tests of knowledge, and battling with Pokémon.
